Frequently Asked Questions


Find answers to the most common questions about the Military Allies Affiliate Program.

It is a performance-based, Pay-Per-Sale (PPS) program where you earn commissions on sales generated through your referral links. Our program empowers brand advocates, businesses, and influencers to monetize their audience while expanding our market reach.

Payouts are processed monthly on the 15th, after a 15-day hold period to manage fraud, cancellations, and refunds. This ensures all transactions are verified before payment is issued.

You must earn at least $20 before a payout is issued. Any earnings below this threshold will roll over to the next payment cycle until the minimum is reached.

Payouts are issued via PayPal, Wise, or Direct Bank Transfer. You can select your preferred payment method during the registration process or in your affiliate dashboard settings.

Note: We do not use Cashapp, Venmo, or Zelle for payouts at this time.

Both the Standard and Ambassador Programs have four tiers. As your total sales increase, your commission percentage—and consequently your per-sale dollar earnings—increase.

For example, in the Standard Program, you start at 20% (approximately $19.80 per $99 sale) and can progress up to 40% (approximately $39.60 per $99 sale) as your sales volume increases.

The Ambassador Program offers even higher rates, starting at 30% and reaching up to 50% at the top tier.

Discounts or special pricing offered through coupons and the structure of membership sales may alter the total sale value on which your commission is calculated, thereby affecting your earnings. For example, if a customer uses a 10% discount code on a $99 product, your commission would be calculated on the discounted price of $89.10.

The Standard Affiliate Program is open to anyone leveraging personal referrals and community networks, with a commission range of 20% to 40%.

The Ambassador Program is invite-only, targeting influencers and content creators with a strong social media presence, offering a commission range of 30% to 50% along with additional perks like premium marketing materials, priority support, and early access to new products.

The program is open to veterans, military spouses, service members, and individuals connected to the military community. Anyone who meets our eligibility requirements can apply for the Standard Affiliate Program.

For the Ambassador Program, a significant social media following and high engagement are required. This program is by invitation only, but you can express your interest by contacting us directly.

Apply online through our website. Eligible applicants will receive an onboarding email with access to our affiliate portal and marketing tools.

The application process typically takes 3-5 business days for review and approval. Once approved, you can immediately begin promoting with your unique tracking link.

We apply a 15-day hold period on all payouts to protect against fraud and to accommodate cancellations or refunds. This ensures the integrity of the program and allows us to verify all transactions before issuing commissions.

Military Allies must report affiliate payouts to the IRS. Therefore, your legal name and address are required for invoice purposes.

Affiliates are responsible for paying their own taxes on earned commissions. We recommend consulting with a tax professional regarding your specific tax obligations.
