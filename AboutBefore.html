Their Vision


As USAF veterans, Miranda and Angela experienced firsthand the challenges of military life transitions. They saw the gaps in available resources and the frustration of endless searching for reliable information.

With decades of combined service and leadership experience, they created Military Allies with a simple mission: to eliminate the chaos and connect the military community with the resources they truly need.

Their firsthand experiences fuel our commitment to making military life easier and more connected.

A Community Built on Sacrifice

The military and veteran community is the largest and most transient subculture, facing sacrifices like long separations and constant relocations. With over 20 million members, these challenges demand a dedicated solution.

  • Frequent relocations disrupting family stability
  • Career challenges for military spouses
  • Complex transition processes for veterans
  • Need for reliable, vetted service providers

Economic Impact

$2 Trillion Annual Contribution

The military community contributes nearly ~$2 trillion annually to the economy. Yet connecting with them remains a challenge due to SEO hurdles and ever-changing social media dynamics.

  • Military families relocate 10x more often than civilian families
  • 25% higher brand loyalty among military consumers
  • Military families spend $100B+ annually on consumer goods
